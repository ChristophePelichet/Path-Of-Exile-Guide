^xFFFF77Gear:^7
^xFFFF77^7For your bow focus on the highest dps bow you can find, use anger/wrath/hatred essence on good bow bases. The best bow in the campaign is the "Royal bow". But any bow with high attack speed will work.
Quiver is the same deal, ideally use any woe essence on your quivers to roll bow damage, then flat dmg/attack speed are the next best stats.
Everyother gear piece is just life/resist and make sure your boots have movement speed. After act 5 you should craft resist on all of your pieces so you have good resistance for act 6-10(Use transmutes, don't waste your alchemy orbs).^7
Ideally don't have life on your chest so you can use the life mastery for 15% max life mastery. this isn't that important, but it's nice to have.
